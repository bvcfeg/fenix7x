How to Update Your Garmin Forerunner 955 Solar

To get the latest firmware:

  1. Open the Garmin Connect app on your phone.
  2. Make sure your watch is connected via Bluetooth.
  3. Go to Settings > Software Update and install the update.
  4. Ensure your device is at least 50% charged before updating.
